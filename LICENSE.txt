Zoo typicality dataset is based on Zoo dataset (see below). 
Typicality ratings were obtained by questionnaire which was 
created by Radim Belohlavek and Tomas Mikula.

Dataset was originaly used in paper:
Belohlavek, R., Mikula, T.: Typicality: a formal concept analysis account (2021 - preprint).
